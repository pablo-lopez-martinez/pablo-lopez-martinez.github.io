Featured Project: Molecular Docking Web App

This full-stack platform allows users to launch and manage molecular docking experiments directly from the browser. It aims to simplify the process by integrating AutoDock Vina for predicting drug-protein interactions, an OpenAI API-powered chatbot for conversational support, and 3Dmol.js for real-time 3D visualization of molecular structures.

The entire system is containerized with Docker to ensure reproducibility and scalability, providing a streamlined bioinformatics workflow.

Technologies: Django, Docker, AutoDock Vina, OpenAI API, and 3Dmol.js
